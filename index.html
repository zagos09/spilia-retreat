Our Story

A Retreat Built
Into the Coastline

Spilia Retreat is not a hotel. It is a private collection of villas carved into the ancient rocks above the crystal-clear bay of Kamares — where the only boundary between you and the Aegean is a whitewashed stone wall and infinity of blue.

The property is deliberately intimate. Minimalist Cycladic architecture, private terraces with direct sea views, and spaces designed for two people or a close-knit group who want the island entirely to themselves.

Three minutes from Kamares Beach, rated among the finest in the Cyclades, and yet utterly secluded — Spilia is the contradiction Sifnos does best.

What's Included

Amenities

Every detail at Spilia Retreat is considered — from your first morning coffee with a sea view to the last glow of evening on the terrace.

Direct Sea Access

The villa sits directly above the ocean. Step outside and you are on the rocks with the Aegean at your feet — no beach crowds, no queues.

Kamares Beach · 300m

Top-rated Kamares Beach (8.8) is 300 metres and a 3-minute walk away. Beach chairs and pool towels are provided for you.

Private Terrace

Every accommodation has its own terrace with unobstructed sea and mountain views — ideal for sunrise coffee or an evening aperitivo.

Free WiFi + Netflix

High-speed wireless internet throughout all areas plus Netflix streaming — for those rare moments when the sea isn't entertainment enough.

Air Conditioning

Individual climate control in every room to keep you cool and comfortable throughout the warm Mediterranean summer months.

Private Kitchenette

Fully equipped kitchenette in each accommodation — stock up at the Kamares market and make your stay entirely your own.

Luxury Bathroom

Premium free toiletries, plush bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer. A private bathroom that feels like a small indulgence every time.

Personal Host — Alex

Your host Alex will meet you at Kamares port on arrival. He knows every corner of Sifnos and is available throughout your stay for any need.
